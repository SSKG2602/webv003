Systems

What We Build

Production-ready systems you can deploy in gov-tech, education, health, community platforms, and enterprise governance.

SPL — Subsumption Pattern Learning

Layered agent architecture that sequences reactive, tactical, and deliberative behaviors with budget and safety controls.

  • Reusable pattern library for common workflows.
  • Cost-aware execution with guardrails and overrides.
  • Traceable actions with step-by-step logs.

Outcome: Predictable automation with clear routing and human checkpoints.

Applies to: Citizen services triage, benefits processing, ops command centers.

Multi-Agent Governance

Coordination layer that sets roles, permissions, and arbitration rules across agents.

  • Role-based access and scoped capabilities per agent.
  • Policy enforcement before actions execute.
  • Conflict resolution and escalation flows built in.

Outcome: Multi-agent work that stays compliant and controllable.

Applies to: Regulated workflows, procurement, healthcare coordination, education ops.

State-Grounded Decision Engines

Engines that keep a shared world state, validate decisions, and output explainable recommendations.

  • Deterministic state updates with rollback paths.
  • Scenario testing harness with expected outputs.
  • Human-readable rationales attached to every recommendation.

Outcome: Faster approvals with defensible decision support.

Applies to: Case management, eligibility decisions, safety reviews.

Context-Aware Retrieval

Retrieval layer that fuses temporal, graph, and policy-aware context for grounded responses.

  • Time-aware indexing to avoid stale answers.
  • Graph linking of entities, policies, and precedents.
  • Relevance scoring that includes compliance constraints.

Outcome: Answers that stay accurate, current, and policy-aligned.

Applies to: Knowledge portals, staff copilots, compliance Q&A.

Monitoring + Policy Layer

Live observability and enforcement across every system.

  • Real-time logs, metrics, and alerts on decisions and costs.
  • Policy checks and kill switches for unsafe actions.
  • Audit exports for regulators and internal risk teams.

Outcome: Continuous assurance that automation is behaving within bounds.

Applies to: Any deployed agentic workflow that needs governance.

Projects

Projects & Experiments

Working artifacts that demonstrate the systems in production contexts.

Civic Intake Triage (SPL)

Problem: Manual triage slows citizen requests and loses traceability.

Current version: Layered agents route requests by urgency, eligibility, and policy with human approvals.

  • Step-by-step reasoning log for every routing decision.
  • Budget and escalation controls for sensitive cases.
  • Dashboard to replay and audit any request.

Policy-Governed Agent Mesh

Problem: Multiple agents act without a unified rule set.

Current version: Governance layer assigns roles, permissions, and arbitration across agents.

  • Role-scoped capabilities with signed actions.
  • Policy checks block unsafe tasks before execution.
  • Conflict resolver routes to human review when needed.

Context-Aware Knowledge Copilot

Problem: Staff and partners get inconsistent answers from outdated docs.

Current version: Temporal and graph-aware retrieval with compliance filters.

  • Cites sources with freshness windows.
  • Filters out answers that violate policy constraints.
  • Tracks questions to improve coverage and accuracy.

Decision Desk Engine

Problem: Approvals take too long and lack explanations.

Current version: State-grounded engine that proposes decisions with rationales and rollback paths.

  • Deterministic state updates and audit-ready rationales.
  • Scenario tests for edge cases and fairness checks.
  • Human sign-off step with evidence attached.

About Dasein

Dasein is an AI systems company building governed multi-agent workflows, explainable decision engines, and policy-aware retrieval for teams that answer to the public, boards, and regulators. We exist to make AI deployable where scrutiny is highest and trust is mandatory.

We ship control layers, audit evidence, and runbooks alongside automation—so leaders and engineers have the same picture of risk and readiness.

Our mission: Make accountable automation standard for civic, public, and responsible builders.
