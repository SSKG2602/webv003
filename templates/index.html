About

About Dasein

Dasein is AI-first and people-first. We build state-grounded multi-agent systems—SPL, APL, and shared-state coordination—guided by global ethics and designed for underserved communities.

Founded by Pamela Cuce and Shreyas Gowda, Dasein exists to bring ethical, structure-first intelligence into real-world use—designed to serve, not just scale.
